Two markets, two pitches

The product is the same. The reason it sells is different in each market — lead with the right one.

🇰🇪 Kenya

WhatsApp is the front desk. M-Pesa is the checkout.
  • ✓
    They live on Instagram & WhatsApp, not a website. Most have a Linktree or a Facebook page and take bookings by DM — so inquiries pile up unanswered and leak overnight.
  • ✓
    M-Pesa is universal. A deposit request over M-Pesa feels normal and kills no-shows. Your booking widget takes the deposit the moment they book.
  • ✓
    Your edge: a real, modern site plus an AI that answers WhatsApp instantly, books the slot, and collects an M-Pesa deposit — something almost none of their competitors have.
  • ✓
    Hunt in Nairobi (Westlands, Kilimani, Lavington, Karen, Kileleshwa, CBD), plus Mombasa, Nakuru, Kisumu & Eldoret. Price in KES; collect via M-Pesa.

🇺🇸 United States

The phone still rules — and it rings out.
  • ✓
    A large share of calls go unanswered — front desk busy, lunch, after 5pm, weekends. Every missed call to a service business is a job that walks to a competitor.
  • ✓
    First to respond wins. Industry studies consistently show the business that replies first is far more likely to get the booking. An AI that answers in seconds beats a human callback hours later.
  • ✓
    Your edge: a 24/7 AI that answers calls, texts and web chat, books instantly, and takes a card/deposit through Stripe — so no lead is lost to voicemail.
  • ✓
    Hunt in growing suburbs & mid-size metros where demand is high and competition is sleepy. Price in USD; collect via Stripe.
